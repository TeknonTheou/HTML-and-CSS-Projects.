Cherith Resort

Nestled along the banks of the Chippewa River, near scenic Cornell, WI, and just minutes away from Chippewa Falls (home of the popular Leinenkugel Brewery), Cherith Resort is your go to destination for fishing, camping, and just relaxing. With tent, trailer, and rustic cabin options available, you're sure to find a setting just right for your "get away" needs.
